OUR SCHOLARS

Mufti Menk

Islamic Speaker, Zimbabwe

Ismail ibn Tata Menk

Menk was born in Harare, where he undertook his initial studies with his father, memorizing the Quran and learning Arabic, Urdu and Hanafi fiqh. He went to St. John's College (Harare) for senior school. He later specialised in Hanbali fiqh at the Islamic University of Madinah. Menk is known especially in eastern Africa and teaches internationally.

Bangladeshi Islamic Scholar, Professor, Writer and preacher of Islam. He write many book on Islami, his notable books are 'Hadiser Name Jaliyati'. He discusses contemporary issues of Islam on various TV channel of Bangladesh. He used to hold Islamic Mahfil and Waj in different parts of the country.

Mizanur Rahman Azhari

Moderate Speaker, Bangladesh

Mizanur Rahman Azhari

He has contributed to Islam and Comparative Religion studies. He is also part of a few humanitarian, social, moral, educational and economic welfare activities. He also promotes moderate Islam. Dr. Zakir Naik

Islamic Preacher, India

Zakir Abdul Karim Naik

Founder and president of the Islamic Research Foundation (IRF). He is also the founder of the Peace TV channel. His lectures are very influential, especially among the Muslims in the subcontinent. He was awarded the King Faisal International Prize in 2015.
