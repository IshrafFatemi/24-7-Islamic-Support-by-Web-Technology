Wearing Shoes

If you want to wear your shoes, start with the right foot and if you want to take it off, start with the left.

Hadith References: Bukhari: 168


When meeting People

Give Salam/Tasleem (Salutation/Greet) As-Salamu Alaykum

Hadith References: [Muslim: 2626]


When meeting People

Lengthen the greeting/Salam “As-salaamu Alaykum wa rahmatullaahi wa barakaatuh (peace and mercy and blessings of Allah upon you)”

Hadith References: [Bukhari: 12, 28]


When meeting People

Smiling / Meeting fellow brother(s) with a cheerful face.

Hadith References: [Tirmidhi: 2689]


When meeting People

Shake hands of each other.

Hadith References: [Abu Dawoud: 5212]
